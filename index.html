Let's not Lose More

In 2016, a record of 29.7 million hectares of forest disappeared. This is equivalent to the size of Italy! We need this loss to decline and start growing more forest areas for the future of our world.

2

Stop Wildfires

90% of all wildfires are started by humans. Preventing wildfires by proper management of forests will not only save the wildlife, but also our forest lands, potential loss of human life and many more.

3

Raise Awareness

There are several ways to help solve this controversial problem. Together, we can provide a better future for the next generation. One simple way you can help is by sharing this and taking action!

4

Donate

We are helping this issue by planting more trees, and rescuing and caring for many injured animals affected by wildfires. Get involved with your first donation today!
